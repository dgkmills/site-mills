My Teacher Dan

Role: Full-Stack Developer & UI/UX Designer
Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Firebase
View Live Site

The Challenge

An English teacher needed a professional online presence to attract new students and manage scheduling. The site had to be clean, easy to navigate for both parents and students, and provide a simple way to view class information and book appointments.

My Solution

I designed and developed a custom website with a bright, welcoming interface. The site clearly outlines the different classes offered, with dedicated pages for each. To handle scheduling, I integrated a simple, no-cost booking system using Firebase for the backend. This allows the teacher to manage their schedule and for parents to request time slots without any monthly fees. The result is a professional, functional, and cost-effective solution.
