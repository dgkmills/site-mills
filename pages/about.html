About Me

I'm a full-stack web developer with a passion for creating intuitive and user-friendly web applications. My journey into web development began after a decade in education, where I saw firsthand the need for better digital tools for teaching and learning.

This experience gives me a unique perspective on user experience and design. I specialize in building serverless applications, which allows for powerful, scalable, and cost-effective solutions. I enjoy tackling complex problems and turning ideas into reality through clean, efficient code.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or planning my next travel adventure.
